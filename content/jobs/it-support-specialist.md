## Why This Job Will Transform

IT support specialists help employees with technical issuesâ€”password resets, software installation, hardware problems. AI tools can now diagnose common issues, provide automated fixes, and guide users through solutions.

IT support specialists become Technology Consultants who:

- **Advise on technology choices**: Helping employees select and use tools effectively
- **Drive productivity improvements**: Identifying opportunities to work more efficiently with technology
- **Manage complex issues**: Handling problems that require human judgment and investigation
- **Bridge technical and non-technical**: Translating technology capabilities for business users
- **Enable AI adoption**: Helping employees effectively use AI tools in their work

Technology Consultants focus on helping people work better with technology, not just fixing things.

## What You Can Do to Get Ahead

**Develop consulting skills.** Moving from problem resolution to proactive advice creates more value.

**Learn about productivity tools.** Deep knowledge of collaboration, automation, and AI tools helps you advise effectively.

**Build training and communication skills.** Helping people learn to use technology effectively extends your impact.

**Understand business processes.** Knowing how work gets done helps you recommend appropriate technology solutions.

**Master AI tools.** Being expert in AI productivity tools helps you guide others in their adoption.

**Develop change management understanding.** Helping organizations adopt new technology requires understanding resistance and how to overcome it.
