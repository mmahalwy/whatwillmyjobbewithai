## Why This Job Will Transform

Performance marketers manage paid advertisingâ€”creating campaigns, optimizing bids, and analyzing results. AI tools can now optimize campaigns automatically, generate ad variations, and even allocate budgets.

Performance marketers become Growth Strategists who:

- **Define growth strategy**: Determining how paid marketing fits into overall growth
- **Identify new channels**: Finding and testing emerging advertising opportunities
- **Optimize the full funnel**: Looking beyond ads to the entire customer journey
- **Build measurement frameworks**: Creating attribution models that capture true impact
- **Integrate with product**: Connecting marketing efforts with product-led growth

Growth Strategists think about growth holistically, not just paid channel management.

## What You Can Do to Get Ahead

**Develop strategic growth skills.** Understanding growth levers beyond paid ads expands your impact.

**Master measurement and attribution.** As AI handles execution, the ability to measure and optimize for true value becomes essential.

**Learn about product-led growth.** Understanding how product and marketing work together opens new growth opportunities.

**Build experimentation skills.** Running rigorous growth experiments across channels generates insights AI cannot provide.

**Understand unit economics.** Connecting marketing spend to customer lifetime value helps make better decisions.

**Stay current on privacy changes.** Evolving privacy regulations affect targeting and measurement. Understanding these changes is essential.
