## Why This Job Will Transform

SEO specialists optimize content for search engines through keyword research, on-page optimization, and link building. AI tools can now conduct keyword research, suggest optimizations, and even generate SEO-optimized content.

SEO specialists become Search Strategists who:

- **Navigate AI search**: Understanding how to appear in AI-generated answers and new search experiences
- **Build search authority**: Creating strategies that establish expertise and trust
- **Integrate search with content**: Aligning SEO strategy with broader content and brand strategy
- **Measure search impact**: Connecting search visibility to business outcomes
- **Adapt to algorithm changes**: Staying ahead of search engine evolution

Search Strategists understand search as an evolving landscape that includes traditional SEO and AI-driven discovery.

## What You Can Do to Get Ahead

**Understand AI search.** Learn how LLMs determine what to cite, how AI overviews work, and how to position for AI-driven discovery.

**Build technical SEO expertise.** Understanding site structure, Core Web Vitals, and technical optimization remains valuable.

**Develop content strategy skills.** Connecting SEO to broader content strategy increases your impact and value.

**Master analytics and attribution.** Understanding how to measure search's contribution to business results justifies investment.

**Learn about E-E-A-T.** Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness are becoming more important as AI changes search.

**Stay current on search evolution.** Search is changing rapidly. Following trends and experimenting with new formats keeps you relevant.
